# We have to join forces - Current status and future European policies as discussed at the One Health EJP conference

## TL;DR

This article synthesizes multi-stakeholder policy discussions from a European One Health conference and highlights the need for stronger cross-sector operationalization beyond human and animal health silos.

## Key findings

- European One Health policy discourse is broadening toward environmental and social dimensions, but institutional integration remains uneven.
- Stakeholders emphasize coordination across policy, risk assessment, risk management, and private-sector participation.
- Conference discussions provide forward-looking policy priorities complementary to formal policy documents.

## Implementation implications

- Operational One Health programs need role clarity and accountability interfaces across agencies, sectors, and governance levels.
- Policy ecosystems should explicitly include private-sector and non-government actors in implementation design.
