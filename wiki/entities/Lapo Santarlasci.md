# Lapo Santarlasci

Economics PhD researcher at the **IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca** (Italy). Doctoral work spans finance, applied econometrics, and machine learning — close to the methodological surface of the [[AI Index]] data pipeline. Listed advisors include Armando Rungi.

Beyond the Index, Santarlasci has published on **the global geography of AI patenting** (2010–2023 dataset), examining specialization and international diffusion of AI innovation with a focus on Europe's position in the technology-sovereignty landscape — a research thread that has resurfaced as relevant context for the [[2026-04-30-ai-index-report-2026|2026 AI Index]]'s new **AI sovereignty** framework.

Co-authors with [[Loredana Fattorini]], who completed her PhD at the same IMT Lucca economics program before joining [[Stanford HAI]] — the IMT Lucca → AI Index pipeline now visible across two consecutive Index editions.
