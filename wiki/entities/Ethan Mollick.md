# Ethan Mollick

Associate professor at [[The Wharton School]], University of Pennsylvania. Co-director of the AI Initiative at Wharton.

Co-author on the [[2026-04-28-dellacqua-jagged-technological-frontier|Jagged Technological Frontier]] paper (Dell'Acqua et al., *Org Sci* 2026), one of the canonical empirical studies of generative AI's effect on knowledge work.
