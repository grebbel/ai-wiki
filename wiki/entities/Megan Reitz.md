# Megan Reitz

Associate fellow at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford; adjunct professor of leadership and dialogue at Hult International Business School.

Author of *Dialogue in Organizations* (2015); coauthor of *Mind Time* (2018), *Speak Up* (2019), and the second edition of *Speak Out, Listen Up* (2024).
