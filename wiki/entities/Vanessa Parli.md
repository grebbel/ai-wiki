# Vanessa Parli

**Director of Research Programs at [[Stanford HAI]]** (also titled Managing Director of Programs and External Engagement in the institute's leadership listings). Manages HAI's industry research partnerships, executive education, policy engagement, and the [[AI Index]].

Background bridges quantitative research and program management — prior to Stanford, worked in management consulting using statistics, machine learning, and data science to advise government agencies, biotech firms, and nonprofits. **MS in Engineering Management and Computational Mathematics from Johns Hopkins University**; BA in Industrial Engineering from Arizona State University.

Member of the AI Index Steering Committee; recurring co-author of the annual report.
