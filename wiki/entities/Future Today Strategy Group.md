# Future Today Strategy Group

A strategy and foresight consultancy led by [[Amy Webb]] (CEO). Formerly known as **Future Today Institute**.

For nearly two decades, FTSG produced an annual **Tech Trends Report** (19 editions). Beginning in 2026, the firm replaced that format with the [[2026-04-28-ftsg-convergence-outlook-2026|Convergence Outlook]], a convergence-clustered analysis intended to be more action-oriented for leaders.

## Methodology

FTSG's published methodology — see [[2026-04-28-webb-strategic-foresight|Webb's HBR article]] — is a **10-step strategic foresight process**: signal detection → trend identification → macro themes → uncertainties (STREEEP + W) → hypotheses → scenarios → bridge to strategy → strategy → execution → measure & recalibrate.
