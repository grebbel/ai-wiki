# Andy Wu

**Andy Wu** is the Arjun and Minoo Melwani Family Associate Professor of Business Administration in the Strategy Unit at Harvard Business School, and a senior fellow at the [[Mack Institute for Innovation Management]] at the Wharton School. Strategy scholar focused on technology and digital business.

Co-author with [[Bharat N. Anand]] of [[2026-04-28-anand-wu-genai-playbook|"The Gen AI Playbook for Organizations"]] in [[Harvard Business Review]] Nov–Dec 2025.
