# Aaron "Ronnie" Chatterji

Mark Burgess and Lisa Benson-Burgess Professor of Business and Public Policy at **Duke University**; **Chief Economist at [[OpenAI]]**.

A cross-organisational figure: combines academic work on technology and policy with leadership of OpenAI's economic research function. The OpenAI Economic Research team is OpenAI's counterpart to Anthropic's Economic Index initiative — see [[Anthropic Economic Index]] for the parallel.
