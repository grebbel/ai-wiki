# Terah Lyons

AI-policy practitioner. **Founding Executive Director of the Partnership on AI** (PAI) — the multi-stakeholder nonprofit founded by Amazon, Apple, DeepMind, Facebook, Google, IBM, Microsoft, the ACLU, OpenAI, and the MacArthur Foundation to address the societal implications of machine intelligence.

Prior to PAI, **Policy Advisor to the U.S. Chief Technology Officer at the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP)** under the Obama administration; led a portfolio covering AI, robotics, and intelligent transportation. **Co-directed the White House Future of Artificial Intelligence Initiative**, the cross-stakeholder consultation that produced the 2016 *Preparing for the Future of Artificial Intelligence* and *National Artificial Intelligence Research and Development Strategic Plan* reports — early-canonical anchors for U.S. federal AI policy.

Now adjacent to multiple governance-and-policy initiatives (CNAS, Obama Foundation, Chatham House) and a member of the [[AI Index]] Steering Committee.
