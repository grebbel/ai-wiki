# Loredana Fattorini

**Research Manager at [[Stanford HAI]]**, where she spearheads the [[AI Index]] in close collaboration with the AI Index Lead. Leads a team of students and researchers driving the data analysis behind the annual Index report and the **Global AI Vibrancy Tool (GVT)** — an interactive visualization suite comparing AI vibrancy across 36 countries on 42 indicators in 8 pillars.

PhD in Applied Economics from the **IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca** (the same Italian doctoral programme as co-author [[Lapo Santarlasci]]); empirical research in industrial organization and international trade. BA and MA in Economics with honours from the University of Pisa and Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna. Contributor to the AI Index annual report from the 2023 edition onward.
