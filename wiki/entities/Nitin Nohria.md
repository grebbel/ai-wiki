# Nitin Nohria

George F. Baker Jr. and Distinguished Service University Professor at Harvard. Served as the **10th dean of Harvard Business School** from 2010 to 2020. Long-tenured management researcher; recurring HBR author.

## Appears in this wiki via

- [[2026-05-05-nishar-nohria-end-of-one-size-fits-all]] — co-author with [[Deep Nishar]] of *The End of One-Size-Fits-All Enterprise Software* (HBR.org Digital, 23 April 2026). Argues that [[generative-ai]] dissolves the economic logic of standardized [[enterprise-ai-adoption|enterprise software]]; introduces the Build / Compose / Collaborate / Buy Outcomes four-model framework for the firm-boundary decision.
