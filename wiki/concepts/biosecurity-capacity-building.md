# biosecurity-capacity-building

Biosecurity capacity building combines technical controls, training pipelines, legal alignment, and institutional governance into a sustained risk-reduction capability.

## Why it matters

The corpus repeatedly shows that tools or policies alone are insufficient without implementation capacity and update cycles.
