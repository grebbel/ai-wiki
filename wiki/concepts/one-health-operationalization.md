# one-health-operationalization

One Health operationalization translates cross-sector intent into repeatable structures, workflows, and accountability mechanisms across human, animal, and environmental domains.

## Why it matters

Most sources in this corpus describe implementation friction not as theory failure, but as coordination and institutionalization failure.
