# pathogen-inventory-systems

A pathogen inventory system is a national oversight capability that identifies where high-risk pathogens are stored, under what controls, and with which reporting responsibilities.

## Why it matters

This concept connects operational biosafety and biosecurity work with legal compliance requirements and incident preparedness.
